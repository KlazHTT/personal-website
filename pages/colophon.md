This URL has been my online home for many years and acts as a central hub for my online identity, communication, and work. It was once a personal blog, but I found more enjoyment with keeping an analog journal and using a fountain pen to articulate and express my thoughts.

Currently, I use this space as a playground to keep up-to-date and experiment with web design practices. I like obsessing over pixel-sized details while improving interactions and accesibility.

## Technology

- Coded with [Zed](https://zed.dev/). Previously with [Visual Studio Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/), [Sublime Text](https://www.sublimetext.com/), [Neovim](https://neovim.io/).
- Built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/). Previously on [Carrd](https://carrd.co/), [Bear](https://bearblog.dev/), [Ghost](https://ghost.org/).
- Deployed on [GitHub Pages](https://docs.github.com/en/pages).
- Using [system fonts](https://systemfontstack.com/) for performance's sake.
- Using [Umami](https://umami.is/) for basic analytics.
